Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Udyam registration and this tool

How to check Udyam registration status online?

To check your Udyam registration status, enter your 19-character Udyam Registration Number in the format UDYAM-[State Code]-[District Code]-[7 Digit Number] in the form above. Optionally enter your registered mobile number for better accuracy, check the authorization box, and click "Check Registration Status". Your enterprise details will appear instantly.

How to download the MSME Udyam certificate?

After successfully checking your registration status (and the status shows Active), scroll down to the Certificate section. Click the "Download Certificate PDF" button to save a PDF copy to your device. You can also click "Print Certificate" to directly print it. Note: This is an unofficial reference certificate ‚Äî the official certificate can be downloaded from udyamregistration.gov.in.

Is this certificate valid for bank loans and government tenders?

The certificate generated by this tool is an unofficial reference document for personal use. For formal bank loan applications, tenders, or subsidy claims, you should use the official Udyam Registration Certificate downloaded directly from the official government portal at udyamregistration.gov.in. Banks and tender authorities typically require the official government-issued certificate. This tool helps you quickly reference your details and prepare.

What to do if Udyam registration status shows Inactive?

If your Udyam registration status is Inactive, you should: (1) Visit the official Udyam Registration portal at udyamregistration.gov.in, (2) Log in with your registered mobile number or Aadhaar, (3) Check if any annual update (ITR/GSTR linking) is pending ‚Äî registrations can become inactive if annual details are not updated, (4) Contact the MSME helpdesk if the issue persists. Certificate generation is disabled for inactive registrations on this tool.

What is the format of the Udyam Registration Number?

The Udyam Registration Number follows the format: UDYAM-[2-letter State Code]-[2-digit District Code]-[7-digit Serial Number]. For example: UDYAM-MH-27-0001234 (Maharashtra, District 27). The total length is exactly 19 characters. Only letters and digits are used ‚Äî no special characters other than hyphens.

Is my data stored when I use this tool?

No. This tool does not store any personal data, Aadhaar numbers, PAN details, or mobile numbers on any server. The form submission is used solely to fetch publicly available registration data for display. We do not log, retain, or share any information you enter. For complete privacy, always verify through the official government portal as well.
